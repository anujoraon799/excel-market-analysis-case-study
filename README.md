# Excel Market Analysis Case Study

## Project Overview

This project demonstrates advanced Excel-based business analysis using a multi-country retail market dataset.

The objective was to analyze market performance, identify top-performing countries, evaluate growth trends, classify industries into growth buckets, and detect significant business anomalies using Excel formulas and analytical techniques.

---

## Skills Demonstrated

- Advanced Excel
- SUMIFS
- COUNTIFS
- INDEX/MATCH
- SUMPRODUCT
- FILTER
- SORT
- Dynamic Arrays
- Weighted Average Calculations
- Business Analysis
- KPI Reporting
- Data Validation

---

## Business Problems Solved

### Q1 – Market Data Analysis
Performed market-level calculations and data exploration using structured Excel formulas.

### Q2 – Top 10 Country Market Analysis
- Ranked top 10 countries by Retail Value RSP
- Calculated weighted Historic CAGR (2018–2023)
- Calculated weighted Forecast CAGR (2023–2028)
- Compared country-level market performance

### Q3 – Growth Bucket Distribution & Anomaly Detection
- Classified industries into growth buckets:
  - Declining
  - Slow
  - Moderate
  - Fast
  - Hyper Growth
- Identified rows with more than 40% decline between 2022 and 2023
- Flagged business anomalies for further investigation

---

## Key Insights

- China and the USA were the largest markets in several industries.
- Growth performance varied significantly across industries and regions.
- Hyper-growth segments showed strong future CAGR potential.
- Multiple categories experienced significant year-over-year decline and were flagged as anomalies.
